Tinker Bell

About Tinker Bell

Tinker Bell is a sassy fairy and major character in Disney' 1953 film, Peter Pan. She is Peter Pan's sidekick, and regularly joins his exploits throughout the magical isle of Neverland.

Tinker Bell's characteristics

  1. Tinker Bell is very stubborn and hot-tempered
  2. She is jealous of other females
  3. At her core, she is devoted and loyal
  4. She can fly and produce pixy dust
